We're suckers for beautiful, intentional and tactile physical objects. There is something remarkable about writing in the margins of your favorite book, feeling the imperfections in a ceramic mug, or marveling at the handiwork of Grandpa's old knit sweater. No matter how hard we work on Lichen's online persona, it will never be able to satisfy those feelings.

   

Eventually, we plan on publishing a semi-regular print edition of Lichen. If all goes to plan, it's going to make a handsome addition to your growing stack of half read magazines and coffee table books. You might even find something worth looking at inside. In order to realize these dreams, we are going to take our time and do it right. Check back Spring 2021 for Volume 1!
